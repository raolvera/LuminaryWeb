Private Dining & Events

Private Dining

Experience Luminary in an intimate setting designed exclusively for you and your guests. Our private dining room accommodates up to 24 guests and offers a secluded sanctuary where celestial elegance meets personalized service. Floor to ceiling windows frame views of our chef's garden, while custom star map lighting creates an atmosphere of refined wonder. Whether celebrating a milestone anniversary, hosting a corporate dinner, or gathering family for a special occasion, our private dining experience elevates every moment.


Work directly with Chef Elena to craft a bespoke tasting menu tailored to your preferences, dietary needs, and the story you wish to tell. From wine pairings curated by our sommelier to custom floral arrangements that echo the celestial theme, every detail is thoughtfully orchestrated. Our dedicated private events team ensures seamless execution, allowing you to be fully present with your guests while we handle every element with precision and care. Pricing begins at $185 per person for a five course experience, with customization options available.

Event Hosting

Transform your special event into an unforgettable celestial celebration with Luminary's full venue buyout option. Our entire restaurant can accommodate up to 80 guests for seated dinners or 120 for cocktail receptions, offering you complete creative freedom to design an experience that reflects your vision. From intimate wedding receptions to product launches, charity galas to milestone celebrations, our space adapts to your needs while maintaining the sophisticated ambiance that defines Luminary.


Our event hosting services include comprehensive planning support, from initial concept development to day of coordination. Choose from our signature menu offerings or collaborate with our culinary team to create entirely custom dishes that tell your story. We partner with premier vendors for florals, entertainment, and audiovisual needs, ensuring every element aligns with your vision. Our beverage program can be tailored to include signature cocktails, curated wine selections, or full bar service. Contact our events team at events@luminary.com to begin planning your extraordinary celebration.
